Below is a list of prompts to create different led matrix effects/animations. Do all of them:

---

**1. Camel speeding up (fart and accelerate)**  
Design a humorous LED matrix animation where a camel on the racetrack suddenly speeds up: animate the camel lifting its tail and emitting a puff/cloud (fart) from behind, immediately followed by a burst of speed—stretch or blur effect on the camel, and motion lines trailing. End when the camel has noticeably advanced along the track.


---

**2. Camel’s face chewing something slowly**  
Create an LED matrix animation showing a camel’s face in close-up, filling most of the display. Animate the camel’s mouth opening and closing slowly in a chewing motion, with a small object (e.g., a tuft of grass or hay) visible in its mouth. Subtle ear flicks or blinking can add realism. Repeat the chew cycle in a smooth, slow loop.

---

**3. Player scored high**  
Create an LED matrix animation triggered when a player scores a high value. Display a dynamic celebratory effect such as stars, sparkles, or a burst of confetti radiating from the center or the scoring player’s position. Overlay a large “high score” icon, number, or trophy graphic for emphasis. Animation should play once, finishing with the matrix clear or idle.

---

**4. Player took over the lead**  
Create an LED matrix animation for when a player (or camel) takes the lead position. Animate a camel or marker jumping forward with motion streaks, and quickly display a crown, “1st”, or gleaming highlight above it. Use color or flashing to draw focus to the new leader. Loop ends after a brief celebration.

---

**5. Player is far behind**  
Design an LED matrix animation that visualizes a trailing player/camel at the far right or back of the track. The camel should move slowly with exaggerated “tired” effects—such as drooping head, sweat beads, or cartoon-style “zzz” or sigh symbols. Consider making the background further ahead more vibrant to enhance the lagging effect. Loop or hold for a few seconds, then end.

---

**6. Game started**  
Create an animated “game start” sequence for the LED matrix: a checkered starting line appears, lights flash rapidly, and “Ready… Set… Go!” displays in stylized text or symbols. Optional: have all camels briefly crouch/prepare before starting. Animation ends as all racers begin to move forward.

---

**7. Game ended**  
Make an LED matrix animation that signals the end of the game: drop a finish-line tape across the display, have camels approach and stop at the line, and animate “Finish!” or “Game Over” in bold letters. Optional: a curtain close, or screen fade out. Loop ends after the text is shown.

---

**8. Someone won**  
Create an LED matrix animation triggered when a player/camel wins. The winning camel jumps or rears up in celebration while confetti or sparkles shoot outward. Overlay a “Winner!” banner or trophy icon above the victor. Animate for a few seconds, then fade out or return to idle.
