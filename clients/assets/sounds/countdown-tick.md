# countdown-tick

Countdown beat (3... 2... 1...). Rhythmic, anticipation-building, clear tick.

## Horse Derby

> Single deep wooden block knock, like a steward's gavel counting down at a British race meet. Resonant, authoritative, clean. 0.2 seconds, 44100 Hz stereo.

## Camel Derby

> Single frame drum hit, like a deep darbuka strike echoing in a desert arena. Warm, resonant, slightly reverberant. 0.2 seconds, 44100 Hz stereo.
