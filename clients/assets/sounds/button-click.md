# button-click

Physical button press on the game controller. Snappy, tactile, instant feedback.

## Horse Derby

> Crisp wooden click, like a riding crop tapping a fence post. Tight, dry, no reverb. 0.05 seconds, 44100 Hz stereo.

## Camel Derby

> Short dry tap on a ceramic surface, like a finger striking a clay pot in a desert bazaar. Punchy, immediate, no reverb. 0.05 seconds, 44100 Hz stereo.
