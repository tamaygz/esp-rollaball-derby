# became-last

Player dropped to last place. Short, deflating, disappointing sound effect.

## Horse Derby

> Sad descending two-note horn call, like a distant hunting horn playing a falling minor interval on an English countryside racetrack. Short, muffled, slightly comedic disappointment. 0.4 seconds, 44100 Hz stereo.

## Camel Derby

> Short descending oud pluck with a dry reverb tail, like a dejected sigh in the desert wind. Arabian-flavored two-note descending motif. 0.4 seconds, 44100 Hz stereo.
