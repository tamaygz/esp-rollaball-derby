# game-started

Race officially begins — transition from countdown to running. Explosive, exciting starter signal.

## Horse Derby

> Sharp starting pistol crack followed by a brief crowd roar, like a classic English horse race starting at the gates. Punchy attack, short excited tail. 0.5 seconds, 44100 Hz stereo.

## Camel Derby

> Loud ceremonial horn blast with crowd cheering in a dusty arena, like a desert racing festival signal. Brassy, bold, with sandy ambience. 0.5 seconds, 44100 Hz stereo.
